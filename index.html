About

I'm a second-year PhD student in the Physiology, Biophysics and Systems Biology (PBSB) program at Weill Cornell Medicine, Houston Methodist campus, advised by Dimitry Sayenko and Philip Horner. My research focuses on using epidural spinal stimulation (ESS) to restore upper-limb sensorimotor function after SCI. Previously, I graduated from Tecnologico de Monterrey in 2024 with an AB in Biosciences.
